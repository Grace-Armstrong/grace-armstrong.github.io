Why Blackjack?

After college I started getting into blackjack and was interested in the edge that players who master basic strategy and card counting can gain.

Why make your own trainer?

There are many blackjack trainers out there, but I wanted to make my own to learn more about programming and to have a tool that I could customize to my own needs as I learn. The development would also be helpful in my learning process.

What does it do?

The Blackjack Trainer is a simple web application that allows users to practice their blackjack skills. It provides a user-friendly interface where players can simulate games, learn basic strategy, and track their progress.
